Milk Tea Recipe

A cup of milk tea (chai) in morning gives refreshing feel and put you on track of long hard day it can be prepared with milk or milk powder and various types of plain and falvored tea powder.This recipe prepares Pakistani tea using milk ,sugar tea powder and water.

Ingredients

  1. 1 cup (250ml) Milk
  2. 2 teaspoons Tea Powder
  3. 1/4 cup (approx 60 ml)Water
  4. 3 teaspoons sugar

    5. Method

  5. Boil water in a saucepan
  6. Add milk and tea powder in it and boil it for 3-4 minutes on medium flame
  7. Add milk and boil it over medium flame for 6-7 or until bubble start to rise.you will see the change in color of the tea from milky shade to brown shade when it is ready.
  8. Turn of the gas and strain tea in cups
